BIO

Dr.Hongzheng Li is currently a Pre-tenured Assistant Professor in the School of Foreign Languages(SFL), Beijing Institute of Technology(BIT) since November 2020. He is also a full researcher of the Key Laboratory of Language, Cognition and Computation(LCC), Ministry of Industry and Information Technology(MIIT)

Before he joined SFL, he was a postdoc researcher(Co-advisor: Prof. Heyan Huang) in the School of Computer Science, BIT from July 2018 to November 2020. He received his M.A and PhD degree of Applied Linguistics(focus on Natural Language Processing) from Beijing Normal University in 2014 and 2018 respectively.
